Privacy Policy

Quill – Smart LinkedIn Replies is committed to user privacy. Our core philosophy is simple and absolute: your data is yours alone.

  • 1

    Zero Server Operations

    Quill does not operate any databases, analytical servers, or remote storage clusters. The developer cannot see, access, collect, or store any of your inputs, preferences, or generated comments.

  • 2

    LinkedIn Post Extraction (Local)

    To generate relevant replies, Quill's content script reads the visible text and author name of the LinkedIn post you are commenting on. This content is read locally in your browser and sent only to Google's Gemini API as described below — it is never stored or transmitted anywhere else.

  • 3

    Local-Only Preference Storage

    Your API key, configured tone, length, writing style descriptions, and custom comment examples are saved strictly on your local device inside your browser's extension storage (local storage). They never leave your machine except during generation.

  • 4

    Direct Gemini API Transmission

    When you click generate, the post content is packaged alongside your API key and sent directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API endpoints. The request goes directly to Google; there are no proxy servers or intermediaries involved.

  • 5

    No Analytics or Tracking

    We do not use Google Analytics, tracking cookies, telemetry monitoring, or advertising scripts. Quill does not monitor how, when, or where you use the extension.

  • 6

    Contact & Support
